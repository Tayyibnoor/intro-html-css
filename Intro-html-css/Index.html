Audi RS3

Audi,s 400bhp 5 cylinder hatchback

The Audi RS3 is a 2.5 litre, 5 cylinder engine producing 400bhp. Equipped with a 7 speed DSG gearbox.

Service Schedule

  • Oil Change 9000 miles
  • Inspection Service 19,000 miles
  • Haldex at 40,000 Miles
  • Brake Fluid every 2 years
  • Airfilter and sparks at 60,000miles

How to set up launch control

  1. Turn Off traction control
  2. Dynamic drive selected
  3. Gear lever on S
  4. Both brake and accelerator pedal applied
  5. Release brake pedal
